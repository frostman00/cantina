Welcome to Cantina Sabers

Since 1999, Cantina Sabers has been manufacturing cinema quality replica light sabers. These High quality sabers have been made for full contact dueling. We began as a one man operation with a dream; now we employ dozens of machinists, electronic assemblers, and support personnel at the largest saber production factory in the USA. We are a licensed dealer and are committed to achieving our goal of Perfecting the Art of the Saber.

Currently we only build four custom sabers. Luke's, Anakin's, Darth Vader's, and Mace Windu's.
